Why are beagles the best dog?

Out of all dogs, you should get a beagle

This is because...

  1. They are very playful
  2. They can be trained-it takes time-to sit, stay, paw, and MORE!
  3. They can sleep in your once house-trained
  4. They are very friendly! Sometimes a little to much that they accidently bite you!
  5. They make you very happy

But here are some cons...

  1. They can bite, hard
  2. You need to excersise them often
  3. You need a fenced-in backyard
  4. They can sometimes wake up early
  5. Unless trained, they could eat harmful things

If you are okay with the cons, and like the pros, a beagle is a dog for you!
